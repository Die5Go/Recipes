Cookie Cat - Steven Universe

Description

Cookie Cat is the ultimate nostalgic treat from Steven Universe. This ice cream sandwich, shaped like a cat, has a soft cookie exterior and a creamy filling of strawberry and vanilla ice cream. It's more than just a dessert—it’s a symbol of childhood joy and Steven’s connection to his past. Sweet, fun, and a little quirky, Cookie Cat is a treat for fans of all ages.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups all purpose flour
  • ½ cup dark cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon almond extract
  • 4 quarts vanilla ice cream
  • 4 quarts strawberry ice cream

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (160°C).
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, beat butter until smooth. Add sugar and mix until light and fluffy. Add eggs, vanilla, and almond extracts, then mix in the flour.
  4. Shape dough into two 1-inch thick discs, wrap them in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for 1 to 24 hours.
  5. Roll dough to ¼-inch thick and cut cat head shapes. Cut eyes out of half the cookies and bake for 16 minutes.
  6. Cool cookies on a wire rack.
  7. Spread alternating layers of strawberry and vanilla ice cream on a baking sheet and freeze. Cut out cat shapes from the ice cream.
  8. Place ice cream between two cookies (one with eye holes) to assemble the Cookie Cats.
