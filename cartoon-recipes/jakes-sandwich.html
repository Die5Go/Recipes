Jake's (Greatest) Sandwich - Adventure Time

Description

Jake’s sandwich from Adventure Time isn’t just a snack—it’s a masterpiece! Stacked with crispy bacon, melted cheese, a perfect sunny-side-up egg, and more, this sandwich is packed with flavor and creativity. Jake’s passion for crafting the perfect bite reflects his fun and imaginative personality. It’s the kind of sandwich that makes every meal an adventure!

Ingredients

  • Long sandwich pickles (as needed)
  • Fresh dill (as needed)
  • 2 hardboiled eggs
  • 1 boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 1 common cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1-2 Roma tomatoes
  • 1 small sweet yellow onion
  • Sprinkling of salt
  • 3-5 pieces of pre-cooked bacon
  • 1/3 cup cooked lobster
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 2 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Salt & pepper (to taste)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 thin steak
  • Fresh rosemary (as needed)
  • Fresh thyme (as needed)

Steps

  1. Sprinkle both sides of the steak with salt, wrap it in paper towels, and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Make the Lobster Aioli: Blend all ingredients until smooth and creamy, then refrigerate.
  3. Toast your sub roll: Broil in an oven or toast over an open flame until the tops are golden.
  4. Prepare the chicken: Slice the chicken breast in half, season, and cook via sautéing, baking, or grilling.
  5. Sous-vide the steak: Seal the steak with rosemary and thyme in a freezer bag, then cook in a water bath at 135°F. Optionally, sear the steak in a pan for 20-30 seconds per side.
  6. Assemble the sandwich: Spread cream cheese on one side of the bread and layer with dill, pickles, eggs, chicken, cucumber, tomato, onion, steak, bacon, and Lobster Aioli.
  7. Close the sandwich, secure with skewers, and enjoy!
