Jake's (Greatest) Sandwich - Adventure Time

Description

Jake’s sandwich from Adventure Time isn’t just a snack—it’s a masterpiece! Stacked with crispy bacon, melted cheese, a perfect sunny-side-up egg, and more, this sandwich is packed with flavor and creativity. Jake’s passion for crafting the perfect bite reflects his fun and imaginative personality. It’s the kind of sandwich that makes every meal an adventure!

Ingredients

  • Long sandwich pickles (as needed)
  • Fresh dill (as needed)
  • 2 hardboiled eggs
  • 1 boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 1 common cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1-2 Roma tomatoes
  • 1 small sweet yellow onion
  • Sprinkling of salt
  • 3-5 pieces of pre-cooked bacon
  • 1/3 cup cooked lobster
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 2 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Salt & pepper (to taste)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 thin steak
  • Fresh rosemary (as needed)
  • Fresh thyme (as needed)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Stretch each pizza dough ball into a 10-inch diameter circle.
  3. Fill half of the dough with your desired fillings.
  4. Fold the other half of the dough over the filling and pinch or roll the edges to seal.
  5. Brush the top of each calzone with an egg wash.
  6. Place the calzones on a parchment-lined cookie sheet.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown.
  8. Serve with marinara sauce for dipping and enjoy!
