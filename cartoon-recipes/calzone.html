Calzone - We Bare Bears

Description

Calzones are a favorite of the lovable bear brothers from We Bare Bears. These folded pizza pockets are stuffed with delicious fillings like melted cheese, tomato sauce, and savory toppings. They’re perfect for a cozy night in or a picnic in the park. Just like the bears, calzones are simple, hearty, and bring comfort to anyone who tries them!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • 2 egges
  • Sausage
  • Chicken
  • 3 red peppers
  • 16 oz button mushrooms
  • Pesto sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Stretch each pizza dough ball into a 10-inch diameter circle.
  3. Fill half of the dough with your desired fillings.
  4. Fold the other half of the dough over the filling and pinch or roll the edges to seal.
  5. Brush the top of each calzone with an egg wash.
  6. Place the calzones on a parchment-lined cookie sheet.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown.
  8. Serve with marinara sauce for dipping and enjoy!
